Recognized an institutional need and co-founded the first and only structured debating club at BMSC. Designed, coordinated, and personally moderated 6 comprehensive debating workshops, training members in logical reasoning, speech structuring, and critical analysis.
Chief Organizer — 1st BMSC National Debate Carnival
BIAM Model School & College · Dhaka
Conceptualized, planned, and executed the largest national-level academic event in BMSC history. Gathered approximately 1,000 total participants, with 830 active competitors from 63+ schools and colleges across Bangladesh.
